Services

At Gentle Hands Dental, we understand the importance of a healthy and confident smile. Our dedicated team of experienced dentists and compassionate staff is committed to providing you with the highest quality dental care in a comfortable and relaxing environment.


Whether you're in need of a routine check-up or more specialized treatments, we offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet your unique needs. Your journey to a brighter, healthier smile begins here at Gentle Hands Dental. Explore our services and let us help you achieve the smile you've always dreamed of.

"Experience the Gentle difference."

Cleaning

Experience the refreshing sensation of a professionally cleaned smile with our top-notch dental cleaning service. At Gentle Hands Dental, we take pride in revitalizing your oral health by removing stubborn plaque and tartar buildup, leaving your teeth feeling smoother and your breath fresher.


Our skilled dental hygienists use the latest in advanced cleaning techniques, ensuring a thorough and gentle treatment that will have your smile looking and feeling its absolute best. Say goodbye to stains and hello to a brighter, healthier you.

Comprehensive Exam

Embark on a journey to total dental wellness with our comprehensive dental exam service.We go the extra mile to ensure every aspect of your oral health is thoroughly assessed. Our experienced dentists utilize cutting-edge technology and a keen eye for detail to meticulously examine your teeth, gums, and overall oral condition.


From identifying potential issues to developing personalized treatment plans, our comprehensive exams are the foundation of your dental well-being. Whether you're seeking to maintain your vibrant smile or address specific concerns, trust in our expertise to guide you on the path to optimal oral health.

Kid's Dentistry

At Gentle Hands Dental, we understand the unique needs and anxieties that children may have about dental care. That's why we've crafted a warm, friendly, and fun environment that's tailor-made to make your child's dental experience a breeze. Our gentle and patient pediatric specialists take the time to ensure your little ones feel comfortable and at ease during their visit.


From playful decor to kid-friendly explanations, we're committed to making dental check-ups an enjoyable adventure. We believe in nurturing healthy smiles from a young age, so your child can grow up with a strong, confident grin. Join us in cultivating a lifetime of good oral habits – schedule your child's next dental appointment with us and watch their smile shine!

Disimpaction

Experience relief and comfort with our expert Disimpaction service at Gentle Hands Dental. We understand that impacted teeth can be a source of discomfort and concern. Our skilled dental professionals are here to provide you with a safe, efficient, and pain-free disimpaction procedure. Using state-of-the-art techniques and equipment, we carefully address the issue, ensuring minimal disruption to your oral health.


Your comfort and well-being are our top priorities, and we're dedicated to making the process as smooth as possible. Say goodbye to the discomfort and uncertainty – trust Gentle Hands Dental for a disimpaction procedure that will have you smiling with confidence once again. Your journey to relief starts here.

Extraction

Discover gentle and expert care with our Extraction service at Gentle Hands Dental. We understand that the decision to have a tooth extracted can be daunting, but rest assured, our experienced team is here to make the process as comfortable as possible. Whether it's a damaged tooth or wisdom teeth causing discomfort, our skilled dentists utilize advanced techniques to ensure a smooth and painless extraction.


Your well-being is our top priority, and we take every step to ensure you are at ease throughout the procedure. At Gentle Hands Dental, we strive to provide you with the utmost care and support during your extraction, so you can leave our office with confidence and a smile on your face.

Fillings

Experience the artistry of dental Fillings at Gentle Hands Dental, where we restore and protect your teeth with precision and care. Fillings are a solution to repair and seal cavities in your teeth, preventing further damage and ensuring your smile remains healthy and beautiful.


Our skilled dentists use the latest materials and techniques to provide you with durable, natural-looking fillings that blend seamlessly with your teeth. Say goodbye to dental discomfort and hello to a revitalized, confident smile with our exceptional filling services. Your journey to optimal oral health begins here.

Oral Health Education

Unlock the key to a lifetime of healthy smiles with our Oral Health Education service at Gentle Hands Dental. Oral health education is the foundation of maintaining a vibrant and confident smile. Our dedicated team is here to empower you with the knowledge and tools you need to make informed decisions about your oral care.


From proper brushing and flossing techniques to dietary guidance and preventative strategies, we provide comprehensive education tailored to your unique needs. We believe that a well-informed patient is a happier, healthier one, and we're committed to ensuring you have the resources to preserve your oral well-being for years to come. Join us in prioritizing your dental health and embark on a journey to a brighter, more confident smile.

Root Canal Treatment

Experience relief and restoration with our expert Root Canal Treatment service at Gentle Hands Dental. Root Canal Treatment is a specialized dental procedure designed to save a damaged or infected tooth by removing the affected pulp and sealing the canal. Our highly skilled endodontists utilize advanced techniques and state-of-the-art equipment to ensure a comfortable and effective treatment. Your comfort and well-being are our top priorities, and we're dedicated to making the process as painless as possible
